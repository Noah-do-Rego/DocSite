Illnesses

Acne is a common skin condition that affects most people at some point. It causes spots, oily skin and sometimes skin that's hot or painful to touch. Acne most commonly develops on the face, back and chest.

Remedies: Don't wash affected areas of skin more than twice a day. Frequent washing can irritate the skin and make symptoms worse Wash the affected area with a mild soap or cleanser and lukewarm water. Very hot or cold water can make acne worse Don't try to "clean out" blackheads or squeeze spots. This can make them worse and cause permanent scarring

An allergy is a reaction the body has to a particular food or substance (such as grass, chemicals, etc.). Symptoms of an allergy can include sneezing, a runny or blocked nose, watery eyes and more.

Remedies: Try to determine what is causing your allergy and refrain from contact/ingestion of it Contact your doctor for next steps to take

Asthma is a common long-term condition that can cause coughing, wheezing, chest tightness and breathlessness. It is caused by the inflamation of bronchi in your lungs.

Remedies: Avoidance of possible triggers The use of medication or an inhaler
