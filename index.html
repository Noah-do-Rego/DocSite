About

DocSite enables users to easily learn about how to deal with common illnesses and symptoms,
recovery positions, health facts and statistics, and more. It was created to be an accesible tool that
anyone can learn from. This is a prototype of the platform.

Vision

The vision for DocSite is to expand it to become an intuitive, globally used platform to teach
and learn about healthcare and wellness. It is currently in phase 1 of development.
